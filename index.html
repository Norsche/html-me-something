The World

"The campaign takes place in broad borderland region known as the Nentir Vale. The vale is now mostly empty, with a handful of living villages and towns scattered over this wide area. Abanonded farmsteads, ruined manors, and broken keeps litter the countryside. Bandits, wild animals, and monsters roam freely throughout the vale, threatening anyone who fares more than few miles away from one of the surviving settlements. Travel along the roads or river is usually safe--usually. But every now and then, travelers come to bad ends between towns."

The Characters

  • Andrys, Tiefling Avenger of The Raven Queen

    • Raised in a temple devoted to The Raven Queen, Andrys seeks to destroy the undead wherever they are found and fulfill the will of his mistress, whatever that may be. As an Avenger he seeks out and chases a single foe on the battlefield, devoting all his enmity to it until it is defeated.

  • Quintus, Human Cleric of Bahamut

    • Once a soldier and now a healer, Quintus has a tragic past that still haunts him even as he travels the world destroying evil while wearing a silver mask and under the moniker "Regenerator". As a cleric, he has devoted himself to the healing arts, but still has a few powerful tricks up his sleeve for his enemies.

  • Splug, Goblin Rogue and Capitalist

    • The Incorruptible found Splug on one of their earliest adventures together and offered him a chance to raise himself above his lowly station as a lackey to a bugbear. He took this opportunity and made a businessman and warrior of himself, so that he could the Incorruptible on their adventures.

  • Ohmphrik, Half-Orc Assassin of The Shadar-Kai

    • Ohm seeks to destroy bad people. Ohm's god consumes souls of bad people, very delicious. Ohm enjoys striking from the shadows. Ohm enjoys playing with pet mouse or pet severed hand. Ohm enjoys shrinking the heads of his enemies. Ohm not so good with people.

  • Sykinax, Eladrin Wizard of Earth-1218

    • Sykinax doesn't believe in much of anything but magic, but that's gotten him by just fine so far. A destructive wizard with explosive spells, Sykinax is the only magic user in the group who does not get his power from some deity. His primary motivation for adventuring is to find new and interesting magical items to add to his collection.

  • Terramel, Genasi Warden of The Elemental Plane

    • Terramel is an Earth Genasi, born on the elemental plane. She has come to the primary plane to learn more about the natural world that she feels so connected to, and to search for the source of a blight that has infected her land. She has a wicked cool warhammer named "Banhammer" that she smashes her enemies with.
